The WorkflowAI Playground is your complete AI development environment, designed to help you build the perfect AI feature without any setup required. 
- Compare up to 3 models side-by-side across our entire catalog of 100+ models
- see real-time price and latency data for each response, 
- get intelligent help from our AI agent to optimize your prompts. 
- Save time with AI-generated test data
- track your progress with built-in prompt versioning
- use diff mode to instantly spot differences between model outputs. 

and more!

Every configuration generates a unique shareable URL, making collaboration with your team effortless. Whether you're a newcomer building your first AI agent or tuning up a veteran agent, the playground gives you everything you need to iterate quickly and build with confidence.
